The Problem We All Face

We get so many offers from different services we use every day. But we can never use all the offers that are provided to the fullest, because we are not notified when the price of the product, we desire falls below a specified value, which most of time, we are not able to meet being individual buyers. Moreover, we also want to avail some features which will cost less, but most of the time we canâ€™t do that as we are not aware of the change in prices which are hard to keep a track of.

It's a pain to keep track of all our expences and even so to keep track of the products and services that we want

Our Solution

Our solution provides a platform for users to select a product whose price they want to keep a track of. This is a web extension that helps you choose the product you want and store its data in a database and repeatedly updates you through an e-mail once the price of that product falls below a threshold set by you. This was not possible earlier as a single user may not always have time to check on the product repeatedly to see any large change in the price of the product that he wants to purchase.

This simplifies our problem a lot, as we no longer have to visit the product page every now and then, this piece of software does that for us and even lets us know when we mustjump in for the purchase
